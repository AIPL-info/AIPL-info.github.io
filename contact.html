Frequently Asked Questions

When is the paper submission deadline?

The paper submission deadline is September 20, 2025. All submissions must be received by 11:59 PM Anywhere on Earth (AoE) time.

What is the format for paper submissions?

Papers should be formatted according to the Springer LNCS Authors Guidelines. We accept two formats:

  • Full Paper: 12–15 pages (including references)
  • Short Paper: 6–11 pages (including references)
When will I receive notification about my submission?

Notification of acceptance will be sent to authors by October 20, 2025. If you haven't received notification by this date, please contact the publication chairs.

What are the registration fees for the workshop?

Registration details will be available on the main conference website by June 2025. Workshop registration is included with the main conference registration.

Will the workshop be held in-person or virtually?

The workshop is planned as an in-person event at the Hong Kong Convention and Exhibition Centre. We are monitoring the global situation and will provide updates if any changes occur.
